# Google Play Data Safety Draft

These are draft answers for the Google Play Console Data safety form. Review them again before submission.

## Data Collection

CalmTide does not require an account and is intended to keep settings on the device.

Draft answer:

- Does the app collect or share any required user data? No.
- Does the app collect location? No.
- Does the app collect personal info? No.
- Does the app collect financial info? No.
- Does the app collect health and fitness data? No.
- Does the app collect messages, photos, videos, audio, files, contacts, calendar, or app activity? No.
- Does the app share user data with third parties? No.

## Local Data

The app stores breathing settings and reminder preferences locally on the device using Android shared preferences. This is not transmitted off the device by the app.

## Permissions

- `POST_NOTIFICATIONS`: used only for optional local breathing reminders on Android 13 and newer.

## Privacy Policy Draft Position

The privacy policy should say:

- No account is required.
- CalmTide does not sell personal data.
- CalmTide stores breathing settings and reminder preferences locally on the device.
- Optional notifications are used only for local reminders.
- CalmTide is for relaxation and general wellbeing, not medical advice or treatment.
