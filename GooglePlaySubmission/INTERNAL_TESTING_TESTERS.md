# Google Play Internal Testing Testers

Use this when Google Play asks for testers before publishing an internal test release.

## Tester List

- List name: `CalmTide Internal Testers`
- Tester email: add your Google account email address.

## Console Steps

1. Go to **Test and release** > **Testing** > **Internal testing**.
2. Open **Testers**.
3. Create or edit the tester list.
4. Add the tester Gmail or Google account email.
5. Press **Enter** after the email so it becomes a selected entry.
6. Save changes.
7. Return to the internal testing release.
8. Review and publish the release.

## Notes

- The email must be a Google account.
- If the save button is greyed out, the email may not have been accepted yet.
- The release will not be available until at least one tester is saved.
