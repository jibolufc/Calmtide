# CalmTide Android Privacy Policy Draft

Effective date: To be added before publication.

CalmTide is a guided breathing app for relaxation and general wellbeing.

## Information We Collect

CalmTide does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email address, location, contacts, photos, or payment information.

## Local Settings

CalmTide stores your breathing settings and reminder preferences locally on your device. These settings are used to run breathing sessions and optional reminders.

## Notifications

If you enable reminders, CalmTide may ask for notification permission. Notifications are used only to send local reminders to take a breathing break. You can turn reminders off in the app or in Android system settings.

## Data Sharing

CalmTide does not sell personal data and does not share app settings with third parties.

## Health Disclaimer

CalmTide is for relaxation and general wellbeing. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
