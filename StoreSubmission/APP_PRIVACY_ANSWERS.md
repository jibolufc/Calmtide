# App Privacy Answers

Use this as a guide when filling out App Privacy in App Store Connect.

## Data Collection
Recommended answer for the current app:

No, this app does not collect data from users.

## Tracking
Recommended answer:

No, this app does not track users.

## Third-Party Advertising
Recommended answer:

No.

## Third-Party Analytics
Recommended answer:

No.

## Account Creation
Recommended answer:

No account is required.

## Notes
CalmTide stores settings locally on the device with UserDefaults. Local-only settings that are not transmitted off device are generally not treated as collected data for App Store privacy labels.

If future versions add analytics, cloud sync, crash reporting, subscriptions, HealthKit, or account features, these answers must be reviewed and updated.
