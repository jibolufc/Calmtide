## Description
CalmTide is a simple guided breathing app designed for calm pauses throughout the day.

Set your breathing rhythm, choose how many cycles you want, and follow a soft tide animation through inhale, hold, exhale, and rest. CalmTide keeps the experience quiet and focused: no account, no feed, no clutter.

Features:
- Guided inhale, hold, exhale, and rest phases
- Adjustable phase durations
- Adjustable session cycles
- Animated tide visualization
- Pause, resume, skip, and end controls
- Optional local reminders
- Settings saved on your device
- Reduced-motion support

CalmTide is a wellness companion for breathing practice and relaxation. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice.
