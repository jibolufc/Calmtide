# CalmTide Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 4, 2026

CalmTide is designed to be a simple guided breathing app that respects your privacy.

## Information We Collect
CalmTide does not require an account and does not ask you to provide personal information.

The app stores breathing settings on your device, such as:
- Number of breathing cycles
- Phase durations
- Reminder preference
- Reminder interval

These settings are stored locally using Apple system storage.

## Notifications
CalmTide may ask for notification permission so it can send optional breathing reminders.

Reminder notifications are local device notifications. You can turn reminders off in the app or manage notification permission in system settings.

## Analytics And Tracking
CalmTide does not include third-party advertising, third-party analytics, or tracking in this version.

## Data Sharing
CalmTide does not sell, share, or transmit your breathing settings or session activity to a server in this version.

## Health And Medical Information
CalmTide is a wellness app for guided breathing and relaxation. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

## Children
CalmTide is not designed to collect personal information from children.

## Changes To This Policy
This policy may be updated if CalmTide adds new features or data practices. The effective date will be updated when changes are made.
