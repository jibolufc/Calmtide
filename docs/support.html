CalmTide Support

CalmTide is a guided breathing app for calm pauses, adjustable breathing cycles, soft tide visuals, and optional local reminders.

Common Questions

Does CalmTide require an account?

No. CalmTide does not require sign in or account creation.

Where are my settings stored?

CalmTide stores breathing settings locally on your device.

Can I turn reminders off?

Yes. You can turn reminders off inside CalmTide or manage notification permission in system settings.

Is CalmTide medical treatment?

No. CalmTide is for relaxation and general wellbeing. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice.

Need Help?
